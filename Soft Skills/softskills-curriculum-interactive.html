Curriculum structure

The UG pathway is organised to move from self-awareness and classroom participation to communication confidence, workplace basics, and interview preparation.

UG structure model

Level 1 establishes confidence, learning behaviour, and communication basics. Level 2 shifts toward employability tasks such as writing, presentations, collaboration, and interviews.

Assessment architecture

Continuous practiceAttendance, engagement, task submission, and visible weekly effort.
Performance tasksSpeaking, discussion, writing, digital tasks, and professional behaviour demonstrations.
Final readinessPortfolio quality, practical review, and interview or presentation performance.
