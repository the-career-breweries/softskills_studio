# Icebreaker: The PREP Method Stand-Up

Welcome back! Before we dive into traditional career paths, let's get everyone energized and speaking.

Communication is the backbone of every profession. Today, we practice structured, impactful delivery.

---

# The Framework: P.R.E.P.

In the corporate world (and in interviews), you cannot afford to ramble. You need structure.

We will use the **P.R.E.P. Framework**:

*   **P - Point:** State your final opinion clearly (1 sentence).
*   **R - Reason:** Give the main reason *why* you think this (1-2 sentences).
*   **E - Example:** Share one quick, real-life example (2-3 sentences).
*   **P - Point:** Restate your main point to close with impact (1 sentence).

---

# Activity: 5-Minute Group Discussion

### The Rules:
1.  **Form Groups:** Gather in groups of 4-5.
2.  **Get Your Topic:** Your instructor will generate a random, highly-debatable topic for your group.
3.  **Discuss (5 Mins):** You have exactly 5 minutes to debate the topic, find a consensus (or agree to disagree), and elect ONE representative.
4.  **Structure (2 Mins):** Help your representative slot your group's ideas into the P.R.E.P. framework.

---

# Activity: The Delivery

It's time to speak!

Each representative will stand up and deliver a strict **60-second P.R.E.P. pitch** to the class.

**Remember:**
*   Project your voice.
*   Maintain eye contact.
*   Stick to the framework!
